HP

HP

Determines each Pokémon's stamina pool — how much total energy they carry into a match. Higher HP means a larger pool, so fatigue sets in later and off-ball recovery is faster. HP has no effect on role assignment.

max_stamina = 0.5 + HP / 200
HP 45 (Jolteon)max 0.73 — tires mid-match
HP 65 (Pikachu)max 0.83 — baseline
HP 130 (Lapras)max 1.15 — runs the full game
HP 250 (Chansey)max 1.75 — virtually never tires
+10 HP+0.05 stamina capacity

The 50% penalty threshold is relative to max_stamina, so a Lapras at 0.58 stamina is still "above 50%" and runs at full speed.

ATK

Attack

Sets shot speed. Also contributes half of shot quality against the goalkeeper (combined with Sp. Atk).

shot_speed = 15 + (Attack / 150) × 15 m/s
Attack 45 (Slowbro)19.5 m/s
Attack 9024.0 m/s
Attack 130 (Machamp)28.0 m/s
+10 Attack+1.0 m/s shot speed

Shot quality = (Atk + Sp.Atk) / 2 vs GK's save quality. Stamina does NOT affect shot speed.

DEF

Defense

Tackle win rate and save quality. Defense plus Speed together determine how well a player wins the ball.

tackle_score = Def × 0.6 + Speed × 0.4 win_prob = tackle_score / (tackle_score + carrier_speed)
Def 60, Spd 70 vs carrier Spd 8046% win
Def 100, Spd 70 vs carrier Spd 8054% win
Def 130, Spd 70 vs carrier Spd 8059% win
+10 Defense+6 to tackle score

GK save quality = (Def + Sp.Def) / 2. Goalkeepers get a built-in 10× multiplier — targeting ~9% conversion rate at equal stats.

Sp.A

Sp. Attack

Pass speed and comfortable passing range. Also contributes half of shot quality alongside Attack.

pass_speed = 10 + (Sp.Atk / 150) × 10 m/s comfort_range = Sp.Atk × 0.5 m
Sp.Atk 5013.3 m/s, 25 m range
Sp.Atk 10016.7 m/s, 50 m range
Sp.Atk 135 (Alakazam)19.0 m/s, 67 m range
+10 Sp.Atk+0.67 m/s, +5 m range

Stamina does NOT affect pass speed — only passing accuracy degrades.

Sp.D

Sp. Defense

Paired with Defense to form save quality for goalkeepers. Also contributes half of shot quality when attacking.

save_quality = (Def + Sp.Def) / 2 save_prob = (save_q × 10) / (shot_q + save_q × 10)
save_q 60 vs shot_q 10086% saves
save_q 80 vs shot_q 10089% saves
save_q 100 vs shot_q 10091% saves
+10 Sp.Def (GK)+5 to save quality

The 10× multiplier targets ~9% conversion at equal stats — GK stat quality still matters at the margins.

SPE

Speed

Field movement speed — the most universally impactful stat. Controls how fast a player reaches the ball, tracks runners, and retreats.

field_speed = 4.0 + (Speed / 150) × 6.0 m/s
Speed 30 (Snorlax)5.2 m/s
Speed 90 (Pikachu)7.6 m/s
Speed 130 (Aerodactyl)9.2 m/s
Speed 150 (Electrode)10.0 m/s
+10 Speed+0.4 m/s on pitch

Also contributes 40% to tackle defense (tracking). Stamina loss reduces effective speed below 50%.

STA

Stamina

Current energy as a fraction of max_stamina (set by HP). Penalty kicks in when relative stamina drops below 50% of the Pokémon's own pool — not a fixed 0.5 threshold.

relative = stamina / max_stamina factor = 1.0 (relative ≥ 0.5) factor = 0.4 + 0.6 × (relative/0.5) (relative < 0.5)
Relative 50% (threshold)×1.0 — no penalty
Relative 25%×0.7 — 30% stat penalty
Relative 0%×0.4 — 60% stat penalty
Ball carrier drain2× base rate
Off-ball recovery0.5 × base × (HP/65) per tick

Half-time fully restores stamina to max_stamina. High-HP Pokémon off-ball barely lose stamina at all — low-HP ones accumulate fatigue throughout.
